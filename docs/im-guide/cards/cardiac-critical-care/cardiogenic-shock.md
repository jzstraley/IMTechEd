## Cardiogenic Shock
- Persistent hypotension (SBP <90 mmHg or MAP mmHg below baseline)
- Cardiac Index <1.8 L/min/m^2 without support (<2.2 L/min/m^2 with support)
- Adequate or elevated filling pressure (LVEDP >18 mmHg, RVEDP >10 mmHg)
